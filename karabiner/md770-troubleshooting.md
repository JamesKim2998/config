# Mistel Barocco MD770 BT Troubleshooting

> **Related:** [[README.md]], `barocco_md770.pdf`

## Key Not Responding (Bluetooth only, works on USB)

1. Check layout hasn't changed: **PN + '** cycles QWERTY → Colemak → Dvorak (tap until back to QWERTY)
2. Full reset: **hold both Alt keys ~5 sec** (LED1 flashes white 5x) — wipes all layers, macros, and remaps

## Bluetooth Pairing (3 devices max)

- **PN + F1/F2/F3** hold ~3 sec to pair (LED2 flashes blue)
- **PN + F1/F2/F3** tap to switch device
- **PN + 1** → Bluetooth mode, **PN + 2** → USB mode
